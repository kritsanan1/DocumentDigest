# Master Prompts for No-Code Local Government App (LocalGov Thailand)

## Objective
Develop a no-code mobile/web application to support Thai local administrative organizations (e.g., อบต., ทต., ทม., ทน., อบจ.) in delivering services, managing data, and engaging citizens, with a user-friendly design and sustainable revenue potential.

## Workflow
1. **User Interface**
   - Header with "LocalGov Thailand" and status (e.g., "Services Active").
   - Navigation tabs: "Home", "Services", "Reports", "Announcements", "Profile".
   - On "Home" tab: Welcome message, quick links to common services, and news feed.
   - On "Services" tab: Access to services like tax payment, permit requests, and complaint filing.
   - On "Reports" tab: View financial or community data (e.g., budget, waste management).
   - On "Announcements" tab: Display local news, events, and emergency alerts.
   - On "Profile" tab: Citizen registration, service history, and feedback submission.

2. **Features**
   - **Service Access**: Enable tax payments, permit applications (e.g., building), and complaint submissions with status tracking.
   - **Data Management**: Provide dashboards for financial records, waste management, and community statistics (inspired by e-LAAS system).
   - **Citizen Engagement**: Allow feedback, polls, and real-time chat with local officials.
   - **Notifications**: Send alerts for service updates, payment deadlines, or local events.
   - **Authentication**: Secure login with Thai ID or ThaID integration for citizens.
   - **Multilingual**: Support Thai and English for broader accessibility.

3. **Design**
   - Use a professional theme with green (#00FF00) and white on a neutral background (#F0F0F0).
   - Apply clean card layouts for services and reports.
   - Ensure mobile-responsive design with large, accessible buttons.

4. **Data Structure**
   - **Citizens**: Fields - Thai ID, Name, Address, Phone, Service History.
   - **Services**: Fields - Service Type (Tax, Permit, Complaint), Status, Date, Citizen ID.
   - **Reports**: Fields - Category (Finance, Waste), Data, Date, Organization ID.
   - **Announcements**: Fields - Title, Content, Date, Category (News, Emergency).

5. **Integrations**
   - **ThaID/D.DOPA**: Use API or manual sync for citizen verification (refer to DGA’s ทางรัฐ app).
   - **Payment**: Integrate with Krungthai Bank or local payment gateways.
   - **Notifications**: Use OneSignal or built-in push tools.
   - **Data**: Connect to existing systems like e-LAAS (if accessible) or manual CSV upload.

6. **Actions**
   - On "Request Service": Submit form, assign tracking ID, notify citizen.
   - On "Pay Tax": Process payment, update status, send receipt.
   - On "View Report": Display data based on user role (citizen/official).
   - On "Post Announcement": Publish to all users with optional push notification.

## Revenue Strategies
1. **Subscription for Premium Features**
   - Free basic access for citizens.
   - Premium for อปท. ($5-10/month) for advanced analytics, custom reports, and priority support.

2. **Service Fees**
   - Charge a small transaction fee (e.g., 1-2% on tax payments or permits) processed via no-code payment plugins.

3. **Advertising**
   - Display non-intrusive ads for local businesses (e.g., shops, services) with $0.50-$2 per 1,000 impressions.

4. **Partnerships**
   - Collaborate with DGA or private firms for data integration, sharing a percentage of cost savings (e.g., 5-10%).

5. **Data Insights**
   - Sell anonymized community data insights to อปท. for planning (e.g., $20/report) with strict privacy compliance.

## Notes
- Partner with DGA (www.dga.or.th) or local อปท. for legal compliance and data access.
- Ensure PDPA compliance for citizen data.
- Test with a small อปท. (e.g., อบต.) before scaling.
- Integrate with existing systems like ทางรัฐ for seamless service delivery.
